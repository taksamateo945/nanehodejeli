David Gardner Biography

David Gardner was born in North Carolina, the USA on May 16, 1966. He earned a Morehead Scholarship and graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. He received his diploma in 1988. He attended Saint Albans School in Washington, D.C. before transferring to St. Mark’s School in Southboro, where he graduated. Both David and his brother were solely interested in learning about money management and were quickly drawn to Wall Street, where they became formidable competitors.
