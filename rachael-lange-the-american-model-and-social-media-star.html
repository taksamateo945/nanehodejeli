Rachael Lange Early Life 

Rachael Lange was born in the United States of America. Her parents are Michael Lange and Shirley Lange. She grew up in New York City, New York, and currently resides there.

Personal Life

In 2023, rumors swirled about her dating actor and musician Dylan O’Brien. This buzz started during Paris Fashion Week when they were spotted holding hands.

Not much is known about this romance, but it got fans talking.

Rachael Lange Physical Appearance

Rachael Lange is a slender beauty, standing at 5 feet 5 inches (165 centimeters) and weighing 54 kilograms (about 119 pounds).

Her dark brown hair and piercing blue eyes make her stand out.

The Professional Side

Rachael’s career is pretty exciting. She’s represented by several modeling agencies, like Elite Model Management in New York City, Ford Models, One Management, and Zombie Model Management.

These agencies open doors to amazing opportunities in the modeling world.

Rachael’s Career

Rachael Lange isn’t just a model; she’s also a social media star. She’s known for her stylish modeling photos, glamorous bikini shots, and glimpses into her everyday life.

Rachael Lange Social Media

She keeps an active social media presence, constantly updating her ever-growing fan base. You can find all of this on her Instagram account, @rachaellange.

Promoting Brands

Conclusion

Rachael Lange isn’t just a model and a social media star; she’s an American sensation. With her striking looks, growing modeling career, and her ability to share her life on Instagram, she’s made quite a name for herself. What’s next for this Capricorn beauty? Keep an eye on her to find out!
