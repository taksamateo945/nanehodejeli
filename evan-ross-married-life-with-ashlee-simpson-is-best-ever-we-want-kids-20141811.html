Evan Ross: Married Life With Ashlee Simpson Is Best Ever, We Want Kids

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Still in the honeymoon phase! Evan Ross gushed to Us Weekly about married life with Ashlee Simpson during the star-studded The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 1 L.A. premiere on Monday, Nov. 17.

“It’s the best thing ever. I actually want to get back to her, although I’m excited to be on the carpet — it’s incredible,” Ross, 26, told Us on the red carpet. “I wish she didn’t leave but there’s a lot going on here.”

“I’ve been with her now for a while so it just feels right,” he added. “I’m happy to say she’s my wife.”
