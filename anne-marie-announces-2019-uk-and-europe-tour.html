Anne-Marie Announces 2019 UK and Europe Tour

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

Following on from supporting her pal Ed Sheeran on tour, Anne-Marie has announced her biggest run of the UK and European dates for 2019. Taking place in May and June the 27 date tour closes up at London’s Eventim Apollo on 12th June.

The tour will be in support of Anne-Marie’s debut album Speak Your Mind which was released in April and has since been certified Gold. Oh, and did we forget to mention that it charted at number 3? Now that’s impressive for a debut album. Next year’s tour will be spanning much of Europe and the UK’s territories so you’ve got no excuse to miss out on heading to a show. For full dates see the tour poster below.

Currently out on her ‘Speak Your Mind‘ tour, the female songstress has already visited the United States and Canada before she moves on to shows in Australia, Singapore and the UK. The UK leg of her tour will see her playing two sold-out nights at Brixton’s O2 Academy.

2018 has been a year of success and great singles including a collaboration with Marshmello for tongue-in-cheek Friends and a throwback with some noughties hit references in 2002. We cannot wait to see what Anne-Marie surprises us with, in the run up to her 2019 tour.

Tickets for the 2019 shows go on sale at 10am on Friday 5th October. Don’t hang about as we reckon they won’t be about for long! Which Anne-Marie dates are you going to be trying to get tickets for? Tweet us @CelebMix to let us know!
