Amber Rose, Blac Chyna Make a Statement at VMAs 2015 in &quot;Slut&quot; Outfits

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

This outfit speaks for itself — literally! Amber Rose made a major statement at the 2015 MTV VMAs on Sunday, Aug. 30, when she hit the red carpet with pal Blac Chyna in a skintight nude catsuit covered in words like “Slut,” “Whore,” and “Stripper.”

The voluptuous model, 31, was all smiles as she posed for photos in the curve-flaunting outfit, which she paired with black sandals and sunglasses from her eyewear line. Blac Chyna, 27, wore the same obscenity-laden print, but in the form of a plunging, floor-sweeping gown.

The duo — who are reportedly getting their own MTV reality show together — explained to pre-show host Kelly Osbourne that they wanted to take all the hate against them and own it. “We basically wanted to paint a picture of what everybody already says about us,” Tyga‘s ex quipped.

Added Rose: “They call us sluts and whores all the time, so we just embrace it. I have slut written across my vagina…I wish I was a whore. My social life would be much more exciting.”
