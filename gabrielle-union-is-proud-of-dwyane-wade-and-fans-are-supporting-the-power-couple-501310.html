Gabrielle Union Is Proud Of Dwyane Wade And Fans Are Supporting The Power Couple

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 11, 2024

Gabrielle Union is praising her man and she made sure to show this to her fans. Check out the sweet pic that she shared.

Someone said: ‘May you and Family always Stay Blessed champ,’ and a commenter posted this: ‘That’s how it should be family that works and understand each other stays together forever.’

A fan said: ‘As you should be. Such success. Such beauty. Inspiring I love to see it!’ and a commenter posted this: ‘You all are doing it and making it work your way! Congrats and thanks for sharing the inspirational journey.’

Gabrielle Union wrote a praising message for Quinta Brunson. Check it out below and see some of the fans’ reactions.

‘Today’s #WCW is the vivacious, hilarious, and gorgeous Quinta Brunson. Always at the forefront of making everyone laugh, @quintab just released a new project that is making school fun again. ‘Abbott Elementary’ is a mockumentary that highlights all the comedic situations teachers and school staff might encounter,’ Gabrielle began.

She continued and said: ‘While other shows enjoy spotlighting older crowds like, high school students – Quinta likes that elementary school feels like an “untapped playground.” Quinta’s character is an optimistic young teacher named Janine who is constantly dodging life’s obstacles all while making the audience laugh. I cannot wait to tune in! If you’re looking for a great stocking stuffer, I highly recommend picking up Quinta’s book ‘She Memes Well: Essays.’ It’ll be sure to make you laugh! Let us lift her up in the light of goodness and hold her there.’
