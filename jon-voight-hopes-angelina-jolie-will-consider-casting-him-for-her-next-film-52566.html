Jon Voight Hopes Angelina Jolie Will Consider Casting Him for Her Next Film

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, January 28, 2024

There’s only one director who Oscar-winning actor Jon Voight would love to work with — his daughter, Angelina Jolie!

“Maybe if I drop the hint loudly enough, she’ll say, ‘Oh, I’ll consider Jon Voight for one of my films,'” Jon says in the new issue of ‘Closer Weekly,’ on newsstands now.

Jon and Angelina in December 2011.

Despite their rocky relationship, Jon insists he is Angelina’s biggest cheerleader — he was stunned that she was snubbed by this year’s Oscar nominations.

Related Story: Angelina Jolie Says She Finally Found a “Real Man” in Husband Brad Pitt

“Watching her film [Unbroken], I was overwhelmed by her God-given gift as a director,” the 76-year-old confides to ‘Closer.’ “I think that she certainly deserved to be one of the group [nominated for a Best Director Academy Award].”

For more on Jon and Angelina, pick up this week’s new issue of ‘Closer Weekly,’ on newsstands now!
