Cardi B Reveals She Filed For Divorce From Offset Just To Teach Him A Lesson And Slams Fans Fo

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, February 18, 2024

No wonder she took him back so easily! As it turns out, Cardi B just wanted to teach her husband of almost 3 years a lesson when she filed for divorce!

As you might have heard, Cardi B and Offset are back together and supposedly stronger than ever a month after Cardi ended things with the fellow rapper and father of her daughter!

But now, Cardi B took to her Twitter account to share a voice message in which she explained everything when it comes to their relationship and her motivations.

‘If I take a break from my n**** and I decide to work things out, that’s regular relationship s**t. If I want to go to an extreme to teach a n**** a f**king lesson and f**king file for divorce, I can do that. It’s my life,’ the WAP star said, slamming all the fans criticizing her decisions.

Furthermore, she went on to also address their worries that she may be in a ‘mentally abusive relationship’ arguing against that idea.

‘I’m not getting f**king abused. If we work things out, if we sit down and work things out and I tell a n**** what I don’t like, what I want to change. When he tells me what he wants me to change, what he wants me to stop doing, we can do that. Why y’all trying to make everything abuse, abuse, abuse. Why? Chill? Our relationship is not only about gifts. If that divorce s**t didn’t went through the court, y’all wouldn’t even never knew what the f**k was going on,’ Cardi stated, asking her followers to ‘please stop.’
