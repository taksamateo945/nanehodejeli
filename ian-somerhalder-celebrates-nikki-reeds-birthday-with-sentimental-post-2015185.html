Ian Somerhalder Celebrates Nikki Reed's Birthday With Sentimental Post

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 25, 2024

May many more wishes come true! Weeks after tying the knot, Ian Somerhalder celebrated his wife Nikki Reed‘s birthday on Monday, May 18, with a sweet and sentimental post shared on his Instagram account.

“Happy Birthday my love,” the Vampire Diaries hunk wrote, alongside a black and white photo of Reed closing her eyes and making a wish while Somerhalder holds up a candlelit cupcake. The Twilight actress, who turned 27 years old on May 17, smiles to herself as she conjures up her wish, while her doting husband looks up adoringly at her from below.

“Me and the world celebrate you,” Somerhalder, 36, continued in the caption of his latest update. “And hope all of your dreams come true. I’m in awe of your compassion to create positive change in the world…”

Somerhalder’s emotional message was penned days after the actor posted a shirtless selfie with his bikini-clad wife from their honeymoon in Costa Rica. In the headline-making Instagram post, Somerhalder again gushed about Reed for making him so happy.
