Rumer Willis Age And Biography

Rumer’s given name is Rumer. Glenn Willis was born on August 16, 1988, in Paducah, Kentucky, USA. She is 35 years old right now. Her father, Bruce Willis, and her mother, Demi Moore, are her parents. Because both of her parents are actors, she has always wanted to follow in their footsteps. Her father was a fan of British author Rumer Godden and named her after her. Her mother had hired a cameraman to film her birth as a souvenir because she was the first child.
Scout LaRue Willis and Tallulah Belle Willis are her two younger sisters, and she also has two younger parental half-sisters. Her parents divorced when she was 13 years old. They remained connected to their children, however, and made public appearances together.

Rumer Willis Childhood

Rumer, who was raised in Hailey with her siblings, enrolled as a freshman at the Interlochen Arts Academy in Interlochen, Michigan. She went on to study as a sophomore at Wildwood Secondary School in Los Angeles, California, after graduating from high school. Moreover, she continued her education by enrolling in the first semester of her course at the University of Southern California, from which she later dropped out to pursue her acting career. She then made her film debut at the age of five, alongside her mother, in the film “Now and Then.” She resumed her acting career with a role in the film “Striptease.” However, before the age of 20, the actress appeared in two films with her father, “The Whole Nine Yards” and “Hostage.”

How Did Rumer Willis Pursue Her Career? Professional Life

Rumer appeared in the comedy film “The House of Bunny” and the horror film “From Within” in 2008. The following year, she appeared in films such as “Sorority Row” and “Wild Cherry.” Later that year, she played Maya in the film “The Odd Way Home.” She is best known for her guest appearances on television shows such as “Miss Guided,” “CSI: NY,” “Army Wives,” “Hawaii Five-0,” “The Secret Life of the American Teenager,” and “Workaholics.” She did, however, play Zoe in the fourth season of “Pretty Little Liars” the same year. Furthermore, she made her Broadway debut as Roxie in “Chicago” in 2015, and she then appeared in season 3 of FOX’s musical drama “Empire.”

Willis then competed in season 20 of “Dancing with the Stars” with Valentine Chmerkovskiy. As the season’s champions, the duo took the victory. Taking the inspiration, they continued their journey and took part in the 40-city “Dancing with the Stars: Perfect 10 Tour” in the summer of 2015. Eventually, in 2016, she broadened her career and worked as a voice actor, appearing in the episode “Yogurt in a Bag” of the stop-motion sketch comedy series “Robot Chicken.” In 2019, she also appeared as a guest co-host on three episodes of CBS’ talk show “The Talk.” Furthermore, in the first season of FOX’s reality singing competition series “The Masked Singer,” she revealed herself to be the Lion. The same year, she appeared as Joanna Pettet in the comedy-drama film “Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.”

Who Is Rumer Willis Dating? Relationship And Affairs

Rumer dated Rafi Gavron in 2007 for a few months. Then, from August 2008 to October 2010, she was in a relationship with Micah Alberti. Later, in 2012, she dated Jayson Blair for a year. In 2014, she began dating Ricky Whittle.

Willis, who is a teetotaler, describes them as a “lifestyle choice.” She has wings tattooed on her wrist to represent her freedom, as well as a feather on her shoulder blade.
