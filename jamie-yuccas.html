Jamie Yuccas Age, CBS, Husband, Measurements, and Wiki

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Comparatively, fewer people of the Journalism field make it up as a public figure, unlike the people of the entertainment field. However, today we have covered an exemplary journalist/reporter who has successfully gained enormous support and love from the public. And that is none other than Jamie Yuccas. Let us learn about her personal life details such as age, husband, and professional career that led her to CBS News.

Who Is Jamie Yuccas? Early Life

Jamie Yuccas is a prominent name in CBS news. She is entitled to being a correspondent and co-host of the widely-loved program “CBS This Morning.” Initially, she joined CBS News as New York-based in August 2015, but currently, she’s working as Los Angeles based.

Nonetheless, her reporting has been broadcasted over all the CBS News platforms. The reporter was always a bright kid. She loved and used to interview and record people from an early age. Her passion for journalism grew right then, which eventually led her to pursue her Bachelor’s degree in Journalism from the University of Minnesota. She excelled in her educational journey with great success.

Age and Family

Jamie turned 39 a few months back on August 3, making her a Leo. She was born in the year 1982. Being born in Minnesota, the United States, she has an American Nationality. Furthermore, she was just around twelve or thirteen years and was in 8th grade when she moved to Maple Grove.

So far as we are concerned about her family, she hasn’t disclosed her father’s identity, but her mother’s name was Karen Yuccas. Besides, their family belongs to half of the Irish descent. She had mentioned that she and her mother used to sell hot dogs around high schools when she was in 3rd grade.

Unfortunately, her parents are no more alive. However, she has a brother named John Yuccas, with whom she grew up. Additionally, she shared a special bond with her maternal grandfather, and she still holds on to the ethics and morals he imprinted in her.

CBS Jamie Yuccas Career and Wiki

Soon after she completed her education, she began her Journalism journey in 2003 at KTTC-TV in Rochester, Minnesota. She worked there for an entire year. Later she started working as a reporter and producer at WBBH-TV in Fort Myers, Florida, in 2004.

Her journey at WBBH-TV lasted seven years till 2011. Meanwhile, she managed to win a Florida Associated Press award for featuring stories about U.S. marshal’s operations. She further joined WCCO-TV, which is a CBS affiliate, in 2011 and worked there till 2015.

After gaining many experiences in the field, she joined the reputed CBS News in 2015 and is working as a respected correspondent at the moment. However, being a renowned reporter, Jamie has been featured in numerous wiki pages which contain the majority of her information; however, she is yet to have a Wikipedia of her own. You can also check her professional background on her LinkedIn.

Is Jamie Yuccas Married to a Husband?

Reporter Yuccas was married to John Sheehan, who was a businessman and a manager. They had tied their wedding knots in 2007 after dating for few years. However, their marital relationship did not last long due to some reasons, and they got divorced in 2017.

Thus, after a decade long of togetherness, they parted their ways. Furthermore, there are no updates on her current relationship status, but there were rumors from some sources that she was dating an airline pilot Bobby Maslar. The two of them were also pictured together on her Instagram.
